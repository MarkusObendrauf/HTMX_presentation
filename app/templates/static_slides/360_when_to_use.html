When to use HTMX

  • A good fit if...
    • Your UI is mostly text and images
    • Your UI is split into blocks
    • You need good first-render performance
    • Eg. Twitter, GMail
  • Not a good fit if...
    • You need many dynamic interdependencies (Spreadsheets)
    • Your UI state is updated extremely frequently (Following a mouse)
    • Eg. Google sheets, Google maps
